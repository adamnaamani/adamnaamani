It is with a heavy heart that our baby received a rare terminal diagnosis. The condition is incompatible with life. We find comfort in the idea that life is not measured by its length, but by its impact, and the indelible mark that is left on our hearts. A brief visitor that skipped the suffering of the world and experienced only love. We have endured an incredibly specific type of psychological torture, and need some space to navigate a time of deep grief, but we wanted you to know. Love, Adam & Susan.
