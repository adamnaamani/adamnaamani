> "_The essence is the essential and the essential is the essence._" _– Michael Green_

Designed by [Michael Green Architecture](http://mg-architecture.ca/portfolio/willow/), the townhomes sit on the corner of 6th and Willow in the Fairview area of Vancouver West. Its minimalist yet richly contemporary style blends into the surrounding environment, capitalizing off of the orientation to the sun with a weathering steel facade, providing privacy, shade, and sound attenuation. Fostering community between its residents lies a private 25 foot wide courtyard between the North and South homes, offering a tranquil oasis within the busy city.

Located just steps away from the shoreline of False Creek, and minutes away from the popular Granville Island, the neighborhood has a sense of community with highly walkable tree lined streets, and views of the city and North Shore mountains. The 25 townhouse units range in sizes from 1,100sf — 1,700sf with a number of high quality features:

- Radiant in-floor heating
- 7.5" wide plank Brushed Oak flooring
- Heat Recovery Ventilation
- Operable glass skylights providing access to roof terraces
- Armony Cucine kitchens
- In-suite security system/multimedia with touchpad panel
- 24 hour camera surveillance
