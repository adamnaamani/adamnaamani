Nelson Court is located in Vancouver's coveted West End. The unit has a spacious interior and oversized windows allowing plenty of natural light. You get partial ocean and mountain views from the large balcony; perfect for enjoying your morning coffee, BBQing, or watching the Celebration of Light. Deciduous tree canopies provide privacy and shade in the summertime.

Renovations include wood-grain vinyl plank flooring and Rockwool insulation to weather moisture, large dark slate porcelain, and vertical blinds. The popcorn ceiling was removed, and the walls were painted a frosted white to give it a modern look.

It’s in a highly walkable area with many restaurants, grocery & convenience stores, bars, and entertainment. A stone’s throw away from craft beer in Stanley Park, beaches at English Bay, fine dining in Coal Harbour, and the Seawall.
