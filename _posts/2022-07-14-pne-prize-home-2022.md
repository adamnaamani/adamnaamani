The [2022 PNE Prize Home](https://pneprizehome.ca) tickets are now selling. You have until September 5th to enter the draw. Located in Langley, this climate-friendly dream home includes clean energy technology, including an EV charger and an all-electric heat pump system to heat and cool the home.

The fully-furnished PNE Prize Home features:

- 3,408 square feet of lavish living space over 3 levels
- 3 bedrooms
- 2.5 bathrooms
- Media room
- Legal 1-bedroom basement suite

The last PNE Prize Home was in South Surrey White Rock. Wisely, the winner listed her house when the market was red hot, and cashed out for $2,075,000 on 11/8/2021, after it being listed for 7 days at $1,899,000. There were about 5 parties that bid on the property, with 2 selected for a second round. Coincidentally I had taken part in that bidding war. There is more room for negotiation, less competition, and a lower risk of overbidding.
