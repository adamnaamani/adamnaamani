The real estate industry is in the midst of a technological transformation. Every aspect from marketing, lead generation, property discovery and transaction management has been digitized to give real estate professionals and consumers unprecedented access to information and solutions that empower them to do more independently.

Consequently, the proliferation of individual point solutions underscores a need for consolidation. Third-party platforms typically serve the top end of the funnel in generating, nurturing and converting leads, yet the ability to manage business relationships with clients is still a fragmented and inefficient process.

When performing contractual obligations, the agent typically follows a system of property tours, open houses, comparative market analysis, negotiation, offer presentation, deal management, and conveyancing, all of which have products that tend to operate in a silo with no means of continuity.

In subscribing to a plethora of disparate applications, a learning curve is often imposed on both the professional and the client, where they need to engage with a variety of mediums for sharing, signing, emailing, texting, and keeping track. This can lead to repetitive data entry, miscommunication, and inconsistent service.

# Enter Rebase

Rebase is the all-in-one operating system for forward-thinking real estate professionals. Where all over the place turns into one place. Point solutions become one solution. Productivity is increased through consolidation, automation, and Artificial Intelligence.

The name originates from a programming term; the process of moving or combining a sequence of commits to establish a new base level. Getting your operation organized so you can spend more time servicing clients and less time managing your business. A platform that seamlessly tracks the activity between you and your client so you can stay on the same page.

It doesn't eliminate the need for the services you love, it brings it all together in one simple, intuitive platform that integrates with them automatically. Embedded in a philosophy of staying lean and agile through asynchronous communication, enabling you to reclaim your time and privacy with a degree of separation from work and personal life.

Trello and Basecamp for real estate.

# Artificial Intelligence becomes personal

As you progress through an agency relationship, your personal AI assistant becomes fine-tuned on every new piece of information. It gives you the ability to extract and parse any type of media in a standardized form and gain critical insights from your data. Files like contracts and strata docs are turned into vector representations you can interact with at lightning speed.

AI offers the potential to help you reduce support time, improve accuracy, and lower costs. Fine-tuning helps models deeply understand your business and augment a model’s existing knowledge and capabilities for a specific task, to craft personalized content based on user behavior.
