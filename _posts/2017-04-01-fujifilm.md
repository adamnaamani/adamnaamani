> "_Photography is a love affair with life._" _– Burk Uzzle_

There is something about the image quality of Fujifilm's new X100F that turns even the mundane into art. Having upgraded from the standard Canon DSLR, I wanted something more portable yet loaded with features, and the X100F doesn't disappoint.

The cameras maintain a classic retro appearance with dials to control shutter speed, aperture, and ISO, while updating the X series with the latest bells and whistles. The new 24-megapixel X-Trans III CMOS sensor delivers crisp, sharp photos with muted colors, and a cinematic vibe to every capture that is trademark Fuji.

Most of the pictures I post are related to street photography, urbanism, and real estate, and the X100F is the perfect device to capture scenes how I envision them.
