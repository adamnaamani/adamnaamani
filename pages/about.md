### Entrepreneurship {#entrepreneurship}
I founded my first business, Resider, a real estate portal focused on listings across Greater Vancouver, the Fraser Valley, and Chilliwack. The company was developed through the Vancouver startup accelerator Launch Academy, where it was formed in a structured early-stage environment, pitching it to a room filled with peers and investors.

### Real Estate {#real-estate}
I studied the Bachelor of Business in Real Estate at the University of British Columbia, which provided the academic foundation for my career in valuation and sales.

Following that, I joined the largest national appraisal firm in Canada as a real estate appraiser, where I valued more than 3,000 residential properties, representing an aggregate value of approximately $1.6 billion. This work developed deep expertise in market analysis, pricing dynamics, and asset valuation at scale.

I later transitioned into residential sales as a Realtor with eXp Realty. In November 2024, I ranked 7th out of 1,740 agents based on sales volume, placing me among the top producers in the organization during that period.

### Programming {#programming}
I began my development journey learning PHP with CodeIgniter, building early experience with backend architecture and server-side application design. From there, I transitioned into modern front-end frameworks, working with React and Vue, which broadened my understanding of component-based architecture and dynamic user interfaces.

Over time, I shifted my focus toward Ruby on Rails, deliberately simplifying my stack to emphasize clarity and fundamentals—working primarily with Ruby, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build full-stack applications with less abstraction and greater control.

With the rise of modern AI-assisted development, my workflow evolved again. I now primarily use Cursor alongside a range of frontier models to accelerate development and explore ideas more fluidly. This has expanded what I can build, allowing me to work comfortably in languages I had long wanted to explore, including Swift and Python, and rapidly move from concept to production across different platforms and stacks.

### Martial Arts {#martial-arts}

I began my martial arts journey in traditional disciplines—Karate, Taekwondo, and Sun Hang Do. While they built a strong foundation of discipline and structure, I eventually found their emphasis on katas and rigid techniques lacked real-world practicality.

My perspective shifted when I moved to Bermuda and started training in Jeet Kune Do. This marked my introduction to a more fluid, combat-focused approach—blending western boxing, wrestling, submission grappling, and street self-defense. The school was directly certified by one of only three first-generation students authorized to teach Bruce Lee’s original system, which gave the training a strong sense of lineage and authenticity.

After relocating to British Columbia, I continued evolving my skillset through Pankration, the ancient Greek combat sport regarded as a precursor to modern MMA. True to its meaning—“all power”—it integrates striking and grappling disciplines, including Muay Thai, kickboxing, boxing, wrestling, and submissions. I competed in the bantamweight division, bringing together my full range of experience into the ultimate proving ground.
